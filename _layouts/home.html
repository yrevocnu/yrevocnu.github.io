Yrevocnu is a creative society engaged in an infinite game devoted to the process of uncovery in all its forms.

The moves in the game are a series of events, held twice a year at varying locations in the real world and online.

They are opportunities to share skills and ideas with one another through play.

They are opportunities to make something.

They are opportunities to shape the future of our community.

Currently the events are invitation-only. We select for great people who we think have something to learn from one another.
